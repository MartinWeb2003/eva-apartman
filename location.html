Kućište, Pelješac Peninsula

Nestled in the quiet village of Kućište between Orebić and Viganj — a minute's walk from the sea, with Korčula island visible across the channel and world-class windsurfing just down the road.

Find Us on the Map

Perna 4, Kućište, 20267, Croatia

Getting Here

  • By Car: Arriving at your apartment in Kućište is easier than ever via the new Pelješac Bridge. From the A1 motorway, take the D414 exit and enjoy a scenic drive through Ston and Orebić to reach us. Free private parking is included for all guests.

  • By Ferry — Orebić ↔ Korčula: The Orebić car ferry to Korčula Town is just an 8-min drive from our door. This 15-minute crossing is perfect for a family holiday day trip to the islands.

  • By Ferry — Ploče ↔ Trpanj: Alternatively, take the Ploče–Trpanj ferry (60 min crossing) for a relaxing shortcut to the Pelješac peninsula, followed by a 35-minute drive to Kućište.

  • Nearest Airports: Most guests arrive via Dubrovnik (approx. 2h) or Split (approx. 1h 45m). Both airports offer easy access to the Pelješac peninsula via well-maintained coastal roads.

Unforgettable Pelješac Experiences

History, wine, adventure, and island-hopping — the Pelješac peninsula packs an extraordinary amount into a small space near your Kućište apartment.

Sport

Viganj Windsurfing & Watersports

One of Europe’s top windsurfing destinations. From our location in Kućište, you are minutes away from schools catering to all levels, chasing the famous maestral wind.

7 min drive
Day Trip

Island Hopping to Korčula

Drive 8 minutes to the Orebić ferry port and reach Korčula Town in 15 minutes. Explore the medieval walls and historic charm of this world-famous island.

8 min drive + 15 min ferry
History

Ston & The Great Wall of Croatia

A 35-minute drive takes you to Europe’s longest defensive wall. Climb for panoramic Pelješac views, then sample Ston’s world-famous fresh oysters and mussels.

35 min drive
Wine

Explore Croatia’s finest red-wine region. From your base in Kućište, visit family-run cellars and taste bold Plavac Mali wines just a short drive away.

20 min drive
Culture

Orebić Promenade & Monastery

Stroll the historic Orebić waterfront and hike to the 15th-century Franciscan Monastery. It is the most picturesque landmark near our Pelješac apartment.

Hiking
